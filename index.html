Our hiring process

  • Screening:
  • At IT Outlook, we do our thorough screening to match the right fit for you. An extensive research begins, the minute you reach out to us with your needs. Each one has to clear our internal methods of qualifying, training and certifying before the match reaches you.
  • Selection Process:
  • Telephone Screening
     Ensures candidate has skill sets for the assignment
  • Personal Interview:
  • 1. Personally conducted interview by our specialists.
  • 2. Only candidates who have been briefed on the project and understand the approach are interviewed.
  • 3. Candidates are judged against the following parameters:
  • a. Technical ability
    b. Good Communication Skills
    c. Integrity
    d. Enthusiasm and commitment
  • Technical Interview:

  • i. Interview is conducted by our Information Services Specialists
  • ii. Candidates are screened for their in-depth technical abilities
  • Reference Verification:
  • Detailed technical and personal references from the candidate’s previous jobs are looked into.
